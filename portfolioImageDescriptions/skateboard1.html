Electric Skateboard

The next 2 pictures are from my most recent project, an electtric skateboard. I built this electric skateboard as a means for transporting myself around campus. This skateboard is powered by 4 5 cell lithium polymer batteries in a 2s2p setup. That is 2 cells in series and those 2 series strings in paralel, which results in a nominal voltage of 42 volts when charged and almost 300 amps of peak current! For the powertrain I used 2 180kv brushless motors controlled with a vesc speed controller. Lastly I built the enclosure to protect all of the batteries and electronics out of fiberglass.
