About Me

I am the product of a Kentucky hillbilly and a Punjabi pindu (type of Indian villager). My name Skyelar Singh Head was chosen through a special Sikh ceremony where our manuscript is opened to a random page, and the name of the child has to start with the first letter of that page. The word was gagan which means sky so as a compromise between my mom's Indian culture and my dad's American culture I was named Skyelar. I was born in Illinois, moved to Kentucky where I spent 7 years, then moved to Seattle for 5 years and have been in Austin for the past 5 years.
